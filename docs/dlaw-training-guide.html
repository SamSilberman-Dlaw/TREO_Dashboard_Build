Section 1

Getting Started

The D.Law Workspace is your daily command center, accessible from the Salesforce Home Page. The Operations Console brings together your most critical information — deadlines, events, tasks, and time — in a single view.

How to Access

1
Log in to Salesforce at your org URL.
2
Click the Home tab in the top navigation bar.
3
The D.Law Workspace loads automatically. A brief blue shimmer at the top edge confirms it is initializing.
4
The Operations tab is selected by default (or your last-used tab).
Tip: Bookmark your Home Page URL. All data refreshes automatically every time you navigate to the page.

Card Details

Today's Time & This Week's Time

Shows hours logged to date. "This Week" includes a small sparkline bar chart inside the card displaying hours per day over the past 7 days — helpful for spotting gaps before the week closes.

Open Tasks — Overdue Indicator

When any tasks are past their due date, the card turns Red and shows the overdue count. This is an immediate signal to act before submitting time at end of day.

Clicking a Card

Each card opens a corresponding Salesforce report in a new tab. No navigation away from the console is required — the report opens alongside it.

Section 5

Critical Deadlines Panel

The Critical Deadlines panel surfaces upcoming legal deadlines and groups them so you always see the most urgent items first.

Date Groups

🔴
Today
Deadlines due today are pinned at the very top and visually highlighted.
🟡
This Week
Deadlines falling before the end of the current calendar week.
🔵
This Month
Deadlines beyond this week but within the current month.
🔽
Collapsible Groups
Click the ▾ toggle next to any group header to collapse it and reduce visual noise.

Filtering by Deadline Type

A dropdown above the list filters by type — e.g. Arbitration, Deposition, Trial, Filing. Select All Types to show everything.

Each Deadline Row Shows

→
Days away — how many days until the deadline
→
Type badge — color-coded by deadline type
→
Matter name — click to open the Matter Preview Drawer (see Section 9)
→
Exact date
Tip: Click any matter name to open a quick preview of that matter without leaving the console.

Completing a Task

1
Locate the task in the list.
2
Click ✓ Mark Complete on the right side of the task row.
3
The task is instantly marked complete in Salesforce and removed from the list. The Open Tasks metric card updates automatically.
Note: Marking a task complete here is permanent — it has the same effect as completing it anywhere in Salesforce and cannot be undone from this panel.
Section 8

Submit Daily Time

The Submit Daily Time panel lets you log billable hours directly in the Operations Console. No need to open a separate screen or navigate away.

Adding and Submitting Entries

1
Click + Add Entry to create a new time entry card.
2
Search for the Matter — type the matter name, jurisdiction, or attorney name in the search box and select from the results.
3
Enter hours (minimum 0.25, increments of 0.25).
4
Write a description of the work performed.
5
Repeat steps 1–4 for additional entries. The Draft Total at the bottom updates as you go.
6
Click Submit to post all entries to Salesforce at once. A confirmation screen appears on success.

Key Behaviors

🔢
Draft Total
Running sum of all unsaved entries so you can verify hours before submitting.
🗑
Remove an Entry
Click × on any entry card to remove it from the draft before submitting.
✅
Submit More Time
After success, a "Submit More Time" button lets you log additional entries without refreshing the page.
📊
Metrics Auto-Update
Today's Time and This Week's Time cards refresh automatically after each successful submission.
Best practice: Submit time daily before logging off. The Today's Time card gives you an instant read on where you stand.

📁
Matter Name & Number
Full matter name and Salesforce record ID for reference.
👤
Responsible Attorney
The attorney of record assigned to this matter.
🏷
Status & Type
Current matter status (Active, Closed, etc.) and practice area.
🔗
Open Matter ›
Navigates to the full Salesforce matter record for complete details.
→
Open: Click any matter name link in Deadlines or Events.
→
Close: Click × in the drawer header, or click anywhere outside the drawer.

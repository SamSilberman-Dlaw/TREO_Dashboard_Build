Section 1

Getting Started

The Operations Console is available on the Salesforce Home Page. Navigate there and it loads automatically — all five panels update every time you visit.

1
Log in to Salesforce and click the Home tab.
2
The D.Law Workspace loads. A brief blue shimmer at the top confirms it's initializing.
3
The Operations panel opens automatically.

To reload data mid-session without leaving the page, click the Refresh button in the top-right corner of the console. It also shows the time of the last successful refresh.

Completing a task

1
Find the task in the list.
2
Click ✓ Mark Complete on the right side of the row.
3

Section 6

Submit Daily Time

Log billable hours directly in the console — no need to navigate away.

1
Click + Add Entry.
2
Search for the Matter by name, jurisdiction, or attorney and select it.
3
Enter hours (0.25 increments) and a description of work performed.
4
Repeat for additional entries. The Draft Total updates as you go.
5
Click Submit to post all entries at once. A confirmation screen appears on success.
🗑
Remove an entry
Click × on any entry card to remove it before submitting.
✅
Submit More Time
After success, click "Submit More Time" to log additional entries without refreshing.
Best practice: Submit time daily before logging off. The Today's Time card tells you exactly where you stand.

Close the drawer by clicking × in the header or clicking anywhere outside it.

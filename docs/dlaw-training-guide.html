Getting Started

Log in to Salesforce, click the Home tab, and the My Docket loads automatically. All panels refresh every time you visit the page.

To reload data without leaving, click Refresh in the top-right corner of the console — it also shows the time of the last update.

Submit More Time
  1. Open My Docket — click the Docket tab in the workspace bar.
  2. Select a matter — choose My Cases or All Cases, then click a matter row.
  3. Add time entries — click Add Entries ›, fill in date, hours, and note for each entry.
  4. Review & submit — confirm the entries look correct, then click Submit All.
  5. Done — entries save instantly; the draft count in the tab clears automatically.
🗑
Remove an entry
Click "Remove" on any card to remove it from the draft before submitting.
✅
Submit More Time
After success, add another round of entries without refreshing the page.

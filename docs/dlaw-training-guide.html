Getting Started

Log in to Salesforce, click the Home tab, and the Operations Console loads automatically. All panels refresh every time you visit the page.

To reload data without leaving, click Refresh in the top-right corner of the console — it also shows the time of the last update.

This Week's Time includes a sparkline — a day-by-day bar chart for the past 7 days — so you can spot gaps before the week closes.

Open Tasks turns red and shows an overdue count when any tasks are past due.

Today's Time and This Week's Time update automatically after you submit a time entry.

Events Today reflects the count of calendar events scheduled for today. Clicking opens the events report.
